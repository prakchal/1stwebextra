  1. Café Nosh, Rajiv Gandhi Nagar, Kodigehalli

    An all-time favourite amongst students, Café Nosh is a favourite, in the list of budget cafes in Bangalore. The café offers decent choice of menu, calm ambience and prompt staff and you get all of these facilities at amazingly wallet-friendly prices. Interestingly, the café also accepts mobile wallet money, as a form of payment along with cash and card. Tucked in the interiors of Sahakarnagar, Café Nosh is the perfect place to chat, relax and unwind over a cup of evening tea or hot pipping cream of tomato soup.

    Address

    No-4, Swara, Stilt Level, Canara Bank Layout, Rajiv Gandhi Nagar, Kodigehalli, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560097

    Price for Two

    INR 500

  2. Matteo Coffea, Church Street, Shanthala Nagar

    If you’re looking for best coffee places in Bangalore, you must visit Matteo Coffea. It is hands down the most buzzing Bangalore cafes, known for authentic coffee and desserts. Famed as a speciality coffeehouse, this place uses the top-quality Arabica beans for the rich coffee taste. Though the food served here is super delicious and varied, this place offers the best coffee in town and has won the hearts of many coffee connoisseurs.

    Address

    Church Street, Indiranagar

    Price for Two

    INR 600

  3. Marzipan Café and Bakery, Ulsoor Road

    Some of the good cafes in Bangalore include Marzipan Café and Bakery, which specialises in Greek cuisine. The funkily done up interior in blue and white colour combination, cane furniture, swing like seats and homely staff will surely make you feel cozy and delightful. There are plenty of reading options, in case, you wish to sit by a window seat and enjoy some solo time.

    Address

    22, Ulsoor Rd, Yellappa Garden, Yellappa Chetty Layout, Sivanchetti Gardens, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560008

    Price for Two

    INR 900

  4. Little Green Café, Museum Road, Church Street

    Looking for good cafes in Bangalore? Specialising in healthy low-calorie food, Jain food and wide array of vegan options, Little Green Café is a favourite hotspot amongst health-conscious folks. From pasta to pizza, sandwich to hummus, you can ask for low-calorie variants for everything here. Along with all these, dedicated smoking area, outdoor seating and free Wi-Fi are surely a bonus.

    Address

    15, Convent Rd, inside Green Theory, Shanthala Nagar, Ashok Nagar, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560025

    Price for Two

    INR 900

  5. 154 Breakfast Club, Koramangala

    One of the best cafes in Bangalore, 154 Breakfast Club is a heaven for all foodies. Its extensive breakfast menu of pancakes, waffles, masala omelette hot chocolate and filter coffee, would surely spoil you with choices. With a not so flashy décor, speedy service and reasonable pricing, this café is surely a peaceful retreat for a lazy brunch on a weekend. This is one of the best affordable cafes in Bangalore that you ought to try!

    Address

    440, 8th Main Rd, Koramangala 4th Block, Koramangala, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560034

    Price for Two

    INR 800

  6. Glen’s Bakehouse, 2nd Stage, Indiranagar

    Good news for those who have a sweet tooth! Head to Glen’s Bakehouse for those super yummy cheesecakes, red velvet cupcakes and almond mud pies. The homely atmosphere and courteous staff have never failed to leave an impression among all visitors. This is one of the best late night cafes in Bangalore, which has retained its fame and popularity for its baked items, desserts and wide spread Sunday brunch.

    Address

    2nd Stage Indiranagar, Lavelle Road, Whitefield

    Price for Two

    INR 800

  7. DYU Art Cafe, 5th Block, Kormangala

    Going by the name, this is an art café, where artworks of globally renowned artists are displayed. It is a typical local house, converted to a café. The traditional décor and homely feeling makes the café ambience pleasing. Authentic Italian and continental food, made from organic and fresh ingredients make a perfect menu for the café. Even, the utensils used in cooking and serving are made of eco-friendly materials, keeping alive the philosophy of the café which is nothing but conservation of environment.

    Address

    KHB MIG Colony, Koramangala 8th Block, Koramangala, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560095

    Price for Two

    INR 800

  8. Mudpipe Café, Hosur Road, BTM

    If you are a vegetarian, the search for a pure veg café ends with Mudpipe Café in Bangalore. Known for cool and funky beach themed décor, straw chairs and hanging lamps, Mudpipe cafe remains crowded in the evening, particularly during live sports screening or live music performance. Not to miss the tempting offers on beverages and sheesha menus during happy hours. Mudpipe Café is among the few pet friendly cafes in Bangalore.

    Address

    Building 4th floor 7th, KDP, 1, Hosur Rd, S.G. Palya, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560029

    Price for Two

    INR 900

  9. Mr. Beans Home Café, 3rd Block, Kormangala

    Featuring spacious seating area, good background music, plenty of good books and indoor games like foozball and Uno, Mr. Beans Home Café is a popular eatery in Bangalore, especially amongst young generation. A bunglow, converted to a café, this place started operation in 2009 and have won many accolades for their unique décor, wide range of coffee and boutique teas.

    Address

    3rd Block, Kormangala

    Price for Two

    INR 1000

  10. Truffles

    The cafe offers the best multi-cuisine options on a budget. Truffles is one of the best cafes in Bangalore and always bustling with crowds. The cafe offers a wide range of dishes including pizzas, burgers, pastas, steaks, and even sizzlers. Featuring a warm and a cozy ambiance, the cafe is decorated with paintings, lamps, and quirky wall art that reflects a dynamic look.

    Address

    Multiple branches including New Bel Road and Koramangala

    Price for Two

    INR 800
